Black's Construction

A company built from the ground up.

Brothers-in-Business

Don "Ducky" Black and Ben "Buster" Black make up the majority of the workforce for the services available from Black’s. This company is your go-to for anything Concrete: flat-work, walls, even stamped work. We take pride in giving our customers the best service available in a timely order. This includes using wheelbarrows, protecting lawns, and being sure to clean the project up in a timely manner. This helps to allow other contractors to get in-and-out of the work site with as little of a hassle as possible. We take safety to the utmost importance. Every-time we cut concrete, or are around concrete dust we're sure to wear masks. Whenever we're removing concrete, we're sure to where safety glasses to protect our eyes.

Serving North-Eastern Indiana for 25+ years.

Black's Concrete has laid, manicured, and kept an eye on many, many projects assigned over the years. We accept jobs from all over the North-Eastern Indiana area, and sometimes even out of state jobs. We take pride in being one of the few companies in the area who have managed to get stamped concrete down to a science. We go as far as to assist, and teach other concrete companies on how to do their own stamping. Butterfield Color, has even sent representatives to work with us for a week or two at a time, in order to get acquainted with what they're selling. Bringing joy to customers' faces is what we aim to do every-time we set out to get a job done. Best believe we'll get it done right too!
