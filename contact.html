Project Information

We stay quite busy, especially in the summer months! So schedule the start date of your project well in advance. A year in advance is a good place to start. This allows for time to have meetings, planning, etc. The winter months slow us down quite a bit, due to inclement weather. As well as putting blankets on concrete, and trying to remain safe on job-sites.

Keep in mind that concrete can freeze!

If the weather is below 0° or rainy, we will likely not being doing anything for that day. (Unless the job-site is indoors, of course.)

Take into consideration, the more extensive the project, the more time it will take to plan everything out.
